      <h2><span style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:20.0pt'><![if !supportEmptyParas]>&nbsp;<![endif]><o:p></o:p></span></h2>

      <h2>Patrick Henry Supper Club<span style='font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:

      12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial'><o:p></o:p></span></h2>

      <p style='margin-right:85.05pt;margin-left:41.45pt'><span

      style='font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;font-family:Verdana;

      mso-bidi-font-family:Arial'>The Patrick Henry Supper Club occurs on the

      first Tuesday of every month, and is a social gathering for Libertarians

      and inquisitive nonlibertarians in the Richmond metro area. As it

      happens, the group meets in Henrico County, and has done so since January

      of 2000!<o:p></o:p></span></p>

      <p style='margin-right:85.05pt;margin-left:41.45pt'><span

      style='font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;font-family:Verdana;

      mso-bidi-font-family:Arial'>Though it is in Henrico, People from

      Richmond, Chesterfield, Colonial Heights, Petersburg, Hopewell, Hanover,

      and Goochland, and a few other localities in the area, are also among

      those regularly attending the Club.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

      <p style='margin-right:85.05pt;margin-left:41.45pt'><span

      style='font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;font-family:Verdana;

      mso-bidi-font-family:Arial'>The PHSC brings a speaker or a selected topic

      to each meeting, exploring various issues on the minds of many. While no

      admission is required, we do ask for donations to help keep mail going

      out and to make sure our speakers are comfortable.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

      <p style='margin-right:85.05pt;margin-left:41.45pt'><span

      style='font-size:10.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt;font-family:Verdana;

      mso-bidi-font-family:Arial'>For the complete schedule of upcoming PHSC

      meetings, please <b><a

      href="http://lpva.com/Calendar/events08.asp?region=Richmond+and+Vicinity">check

      the LPVA Calendar</a></b> frequently. You can also subscribe to the <b><a

      href="http://lpva.com/HTML/emailListsForm.asp">Richmond-Announce mailing

      list</a></b>, which sends out email reminders of PHSC events (reminders

      uauslly go out about three days in advance).<o:p></o:p></span></p>
